Who would you like to thank?

  • Step 1: Send an email to the person you wish to thank
  • Step 2: In the "To:" field of the same email, add "thanks@merciboq.com"
  • Step 3: Write your thankyou in the body of the email

Tips:

  • Tip 1: Be genuine
  • Tip 2: Be specific
  • Tip 3: Be tasteful -- what you write will be published
  • I built Merciboq! as the simplest way I could think of to thank people — My friends, favorite bartenders, mentors, and especially those strangers I meet during the course of my day, people who do their jobs better than I have a right to expect: a barista, the Dell customer service representative. If they like what I say about them, they can leave my Merciboq! public; if they don't, they can take it down. I hope you'll find it useful in the same way.

  • — Fred Schoeneman
